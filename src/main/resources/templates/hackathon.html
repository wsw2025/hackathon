Our Purpose

The 2024 Hackathon is a fun event to showcase your coding skills, develop an entrepreneurial mind, network with other tech enthusiasts, and potentially win prizes! We welcome individuals of different skill sets and backgrounds to build projects that solve an assigned problem within a time frame.

The goal is to develop a functional solution to the to-be-revealed problem by the end of the Hackathon. Throughout the experience, you can interact with successful professionals from the technology field, receive their feedback, gain unique opportunities to unleash your creativity, and meet new friends! What to expect

The event will consist of these parts:
- Presentations
- Q&A sessions
- Guest speakers
- Award ceremony


Your team's aim is:
- Create a functional solution to an assigned global problem
- The specific problem will be announced on April 14, and your team will have 7 days (April 14-19) to create a project


*More details related to the rules and workshops will be posted closer to the start date.

Hackathon Timeline

March 1st

Sign ups open! Sign up forms will be sent out. Find a few friends and challenge the hackathon together, or sign up by yourself -- we'll find a team for you.

April 1st

Sign ups close, two weeks before Hackathon. Single participants will be grouped, and each team will be designated a peer mentor who will guide them.

April 7th

Participants will receive an email with additional information, including the theme for this year's hackathon, competition rules and teams.

April 14th-19th

Hackathon start! Participants have these dates to plan, create and submit their project together with their teams. Virtual workshops will be held after school, hosted by professionals from tech fields, entrepreneurs and educators, to help participants with their projects.

April 20th

This in person event will take place from 10 am to 4 pm , at WXKJ L/5 in New Tower, CIS. It includes a presentation, Q&A session, guest speaker panel, and finally the Award Ceremony.

About Girl Code

The event is hosted by CIS CS council and Girl Code. Girl Code is a student-led group founded in 2020 that recognizes and celebrates the limitless potenti al of girls in the field of technology. In collaborat ion with the NGO JYC, we have successfully taught AI an d a beginner Python course to over 120 girls to foster their interest and proficiency in computer science. Our dedication to promoting inclusivity extends beyond workshops and training sessions, as we actively engage with secondary schools such as Delia Memorial School and Precious Blood Secondary School.

We aim to create a safe and supportive platform for girls to explore and excel in computer science. As Girl Code continues to make a signifi cant impact, we are excited to announce our upcoming collaborations wit h esteemed organizations like IBEL and EmpowerU, expanding our reach a nd providing even more opportunities for girls to thrive in the world of technology.
