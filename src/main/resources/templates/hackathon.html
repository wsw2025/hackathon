Our Purpose

The 2024 Hackathon is a fun event to showcase your coding skills, develop an entrepreneurial mind, network with other tech enthusiasts, and potentially win prizes! We welcome individuals of different skill sets and backgrounds to build projects that solve an assigned problem within a time frame.

The goal is to develop a functional solution to the to-be-revealed problem by the end of the Hackathon. Throughout the experience, you can interact with successful professionals from the technology field, receive their feedback, gain unique opportunities to use your creativity, and meet new friends! What is Girl Code

Girl Code is a student-led group founded in 2020 that recognizes and celebrates the limitless potential of girls in the field of technology. In collaboration with the NGO JYC, we have successfully taught AI and a beginner Python course to over 120 girls to foster their interest and proficiency in computer science. Our dedication to promoting inclusivity extends beyond workshops and training sessions, as we actively engage with secondary schools such as Delia Memorial School and Precious Blood Secondary School.

We aim to create a safe and supportive platform for girls to explore and excel in computer science. As Girl Code continues to make a significant impact, we are excited to announce our upcoming collaborations with esteemed organizations like IBEL and EmpowerU, expanding our reach and providing even more opportunities for girls to thrive in the world of technology.
